A 2000 American black comedy horror film directed by Mary Harron. Written by Harron and Guinevere Turner, it is based on Bret Easton Ellis's 1991 novel American Psycho. Set in the 1980s, the film stars Christian Bale as Patrick Bateman, a yuppie New York City investment banker who is gradually revealed to be a serial killer preying on homeless/houseless people, sex workers, work colleagues, and finally random members of the public. Patrick Bateman is a fictional character and the protagonist, as well as the narrator, of the novel American Psycho by Bret Easton Ellis, and its film adaptation. He is a wealthy, materialistic Wall Street investment banker who leads a secret life as a serial killer. Bateman has also briefly appeared in other Ellis novels and their film and theatrical adaptations.
